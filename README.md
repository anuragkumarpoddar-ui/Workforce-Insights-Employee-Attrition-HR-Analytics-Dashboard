## 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is a critical challenge for organizations, impacting productivity, recruitment costs, and overall business performance. This project leverages **Microsoft Power BI** to transform raw HR data into an interactive analytics dashboard that helps HR teams monitor workforce trends, understand employee behavior, and support strategic decision-making.

The dashboard provides actionable insights into employee demographics, attrition patterns, job satisfaction, compensation, and workforce distribution through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Human Resource departments often struggle to identify the key factors driving employee attrition. Without meaningful insights, organizations face challenges in improving employee retention and workforce planning.

This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing a centralized, interactive reporting solution that enables HR managers to:

* Monitor employee attrition
* Analyze workforce demographics
* Identify high-risk employee groups
* Evaluate employee satisfaction
* Support data-driven HR decisions
